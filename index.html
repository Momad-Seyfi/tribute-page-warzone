About Call of Duty: Warzone

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale game developed by Infinity Ward and Raven Software. It was released in March 2020 as a part of the Call of Duty: Modern Warfare game but is also available as a standalone game. Warzone features a large map called Verdansk, where up to 150 players compete to be the last one standing.

Gameplay

Warzone offers both solo and team-based gameplay modes. Players can choose to drop into the map alone, with a friend, or in a squad of up to four players. The game features a variety of weapons, equipment, and vehicles that players can use to outlast their opponents. The playable area shrinks over time, forcing players into closer proximity and increasing the intensity of battles.

Characters

Warzone features a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique abilities and backgrounds. Some of the notable characters include Captain Price, Ghost, and Alex. Players can unlock and play as these characters by completing in-game challenges or purchasing them through the in-game store.


The Famous Captain John Price is a character from the Call of Duty series of video games published by Activision. First appearing in Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare, he is the commander of Task Force 141, a fictional Special Air Service unit.
